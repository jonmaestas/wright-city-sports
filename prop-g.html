Welcome

On April 8, Wright City R-II School District voters will decide on a no-tax-increase bond to fund a new performance gymnasium and other improvements at Wright City High School. Explore the details below and join us in supporting our students!

Ballot Language

Shall Wright City R-II School District of Warren County, Missouri issue its general obligation bonds in the amount of $11,000,000 for the purpose of constructing, improving, repairing, renovating, furnishing and equipping new and existing school facilities, including constructing a performance gymnasium at the High School?

If this question is approved, the District's debt service property tax levy is estimated to remain unchanged at $0.9995 per $100 dollars of assessed valuation of real and personal property.

What does the community get if Prop G is approved?

Proposition G is a no-tax-rate-increase bond issue for $11 million in bonds for the purpose of constructing, improving, repairing, renovating, furnishing and equipping new and existing school facilities, including constructing a performance gymnasium at the High School.
